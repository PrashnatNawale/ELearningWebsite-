What is artificial intelligence?


Developing computer systems to perform tasks like Visual perception, Speech recognition, Decision making and Translation between the languages.

If we define it in simple terms, we will say making the computers SMART, which is giving them the ability to think and learn without being given the set of instructions and it is the uniqueness of humans that we are trying to embed into the machines.
